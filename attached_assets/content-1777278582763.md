## Featured Products

##### Grease Trap

- Size 22″x 15″x 12″  made from 1.50mm SS 304
- A **grease trap** is a [plumbing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plumbing "Plumbing") device designed to intercept most [greases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fat "Fat") and solids before they enter a wastewater disposal system
- It is installed between sink and main drain
- Great return on investment
- Prevent downtime

##### Self Service Line

- All made from Food Grade SS 304
- Our Self Service Line has an elegant design and attractive appearance with its curved corner tops and its glass sneeze guard, as well as its continuous tray slide
- The Self Service Line comprising cold, hot and ambient units and can be arranged in a continuous line or with corners to suite your requirement
